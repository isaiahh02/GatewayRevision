What to do in Tokyo!

Ueno Park: A spacious public park that is in the Ueno district of Taitō. It has multiple museums. One of those museums is the National Museum of Nature and Science and is filled with some 250,000 materials that have to do with history and science!

Private Tours: If you’re visiting Tokyo for the first time, take a private tour to see the key areas in a single day! Tour ancient and modern Tokyo, including Tokyo Tower, Imperial Palace and more. Lunch at Tsukiji Fish Market is included. The duration of the tour is 6-7 hours and is $102.15 per adult.


Tokyo Disney Resort: This Disney themed resort has something for every age! This resort is situated on two Disney theme parks - Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo DisneySea, plus three Disney hotels and several Tokyo Disney Resort Official Hotels. So much family fun that more than one day is needed to enjoy all of the shopping, dining and other attractions!
