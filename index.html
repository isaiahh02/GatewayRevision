LET'S GO TO TOKYO!

Japan's Capital

This grand city has a population of over 36 million people which is spread over 3 prefectures. It is located at the head of Tokyo Bay. Tokyo is also the most visited city in Japan. The city has a vast selection of tour options! From the nature scenes, to the museums, you will always have something to do! Did you know that Tokyo has the most top rated restaurants in the world?
